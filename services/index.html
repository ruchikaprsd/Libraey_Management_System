Our Services

Digital Resources Access

Offer access to digital resources such as e-books, e-journals, databases, and multimedia content either through subscriptions or open access platforms.

Online Catalog Access

Provide access to the library's online catalog, where users can browse available materials, view item details, check availability, and place holds or requests.

Catalog Search

Allow users to search the library's collection of books, journals, multimedia, and other materials using various search criteria such as title, author, subject, keyword, or ISBN.

User Accounts

Enable users to create and manage their accounts, where they can view their borrowing history, renew materials, manage holds, and update personal information.

Accessibility Features

Incorporate accessibility features to ensure that the website is usable by individuals with disabilities, including support for screen readers, alternative text for images, and keyboard navigation.

Mobile Access

Ensure that the website is mobile-friendly or offer a dedicated mobile app, allowing users to access library services and resources on smartphones and tablets.
